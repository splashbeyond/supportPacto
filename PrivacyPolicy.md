# Privacy Policy for Pacto

**Last Updated: November 21, 2024**

Ephesian 28 LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Pacto mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Promise and Goal Data**: The promises, goals, and commitments you create through the App
- **Notification Agents**: Custom notification agents and their personality settings you create
- **Chat Messages**: Messages you send to the AI assistant within the App
- **User Preferences**: App settings, notification preferences, and theme selections

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and app version
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the App, features used, and completion tracking
- **Local Storage**: All data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData

### 1.3 Permissions We Request
- **Microphone Access**: Used only for speech-to-text conversion when you choose to use voice input. Audio is processed locally on your device and is not stored or transmitted.
- **Speech Recognition**: Used to convert your voice input to text. Processing occurs on your device and is not stored or transmitted.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and maintain the App's core functionality
- Generate AI-powered responses and personalized notification content
- Send you local notifications for your promises and goals
- Track your progress and completion of promises
- Improve and optimize the App's features and user experience
- Respond to your requests and provide customer support

## 3. Third-Party Services

### 3.1 Anthropic Claude API
The App uses Anthropic's Claude API to provide AI-powered assistance. When you interact with the AI assistant:
- Your chat messages and promise-related information are sent to Anthropic's servers
- This data is used solely to generate responses and notification content
- Anthropic's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy
- We do not share your personal information with Anthropic beyond what is necessary for the AI service

### 3.2 Future Services
We plan to integrate the following services in future updates:
- **Authentication Services**: To enable cloud sync and cross-device access
- **Payment Processing**: For subscription and premium features
- **Cloud Storage**: To sync your promises and agents across devices

We will update this Privacy Policy when these features are implemented.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage
- All promise data, agents, and app settings are stored locally on your device using SwiftData
- This data remains on your device and is not automatically transmitted to our servers
- You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the App

### 4.2 Data Security
- We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information
- However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure
- While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:
- **Service Providers**: With third-party service providers (such as Anthropic) who assist us in operating the App, subject to confidentiality obligations
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety
- **Business Transfers**: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to users)

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Access and Deletion
- You can access, modify, or delete your promises and agents directly within the App
- You can delete all App data by uninstalling the App from your device
- You can revoke microphone and speech recognition permissions in your device settings

### 6.2 Data Portability
- Currently, all data is stored locally on your device
- Future updates will include cloud sync and data export features

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 9. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 9. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete such information.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Providing notice through the App or other means

Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.

## 9. International Users

If you are using the App from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States.
